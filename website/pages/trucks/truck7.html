Compact Rental

A midsize pickup truck featuring a 3.5L V6 engine delivering 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. It offers a maximum towing capacity of 5,000 lbs and a payload capacity of approximately 1,580 lbs. Available in all-wheel drive, it boasts a smooth, car-like ride due to its unibody construction. Interior amenities include a 9.0-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, wireless charging, and a spacious cabin with ample rear-seat legroom. Safety features encompass adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking.
