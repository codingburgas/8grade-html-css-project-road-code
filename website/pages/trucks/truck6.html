Compact Rental

A midsize pickup truck featuring a 3.8L V6 engine delivering 310 horsepower. It offers a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,150 lbs and a payload capacity ranging from 1,020 to 1,620 lbs, depending on configuration. The truck is available in King Cab and Crew Cab styles, with rear-wheel and four-wheel drive options. Interior features include a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and available leather seating. Safety technologies encompass Lane Departure Warning, Blind Spot Warning, and Intelligent Cruise Control.
