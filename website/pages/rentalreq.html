What You Need to Know Before Renting a Car.


    Rental Requirements

  • Bring a valid license, be of age (usually 21+), and have a credit card ready.

    • Insurance Options

  • Make sure you’re covered—check your personal insurance or credit card first.

    • Rental Terms

  • Watch for mileage limits, fuel rules, and return location policies.

    • Vehicle Inspection

  • Snap pics of any damage before driving off—better safe than sorry.

    • Additional Fees

  • Be aware of charges for extra drivers, young renters, tolls, and add-ons.
